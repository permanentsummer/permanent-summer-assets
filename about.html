About Us

Get to know us a bit more

So… who's behind this?

Proudly family-run.
Born under the Algarve sun.

Permanent Summer is a small, family-run brand born in Altura, in the eastern Algarve — where the summers are long, the light is golden, and the sea is always close. Christopher is Mauritian by origin and Portuguese by heart, and that double dose of sunshine runs through everything we make.

We're not a corporation. We're not a trend machine. We're a family that loves summer, believes in quality, and thinks clothing should feel as good as a warm afternoon with nowhere to be.

How this all began?

From a garage.
To your wardrobe.

It started with frustration. Christopher kept looking for simple, well-made summer pieces — nothing loud, nothing cheap, nothing that falls apart after three washes. He couldn't find them. So he made them.

What began as a side project in the family garage in Altura quickly became something real. A brand built on one idea: that summer isn't just a season, it's a feeling worth wearing every single day — whether you're in the Algarve, Mauritius, or anywhere the sun shows up.

What do we actually make?

Designed with intention.
Made to last summers.

Every Permanent Summer piece is built around one small, deliberate detail: our embroidered heart. It's not a logo for show — it's a mark. A reminder that you chose something made with care, not just speed.

Our designs are created digitally, refined obsessively, and produced in small runs using organic and sustainable cotton. Heavyweight tees that don't shrink. Caps that don't fall apart. Bucket hats that look better with every wear. No fast fashion. No shortcuts.

What's our manifesto?

Summer is a feeling,
not a forecast.

Summer always comes back. That's the thing about it — even in the darkest January, you know it's coming. That certainty, that warmth on the horizon, is what Permanent Summer is built on.

We believe in slowing down. In wearing something simple that makes you feel good. In the kind of day where you have nowhere to be and the sun has nowhere to go. That's the energy we put into every piece. Sun. Salt. Simplicity. Always.

What really matters to us…

Rooted in the Algarve

Altura is our home. The light here, the pace, the sea — it shapes every decision we make. We're not chasing trends from a city. We're drawing inspiration from a coastline.

Design with warmth

Not everything needs to be ironic or edgy. Sometimes a clean tee with a small embroidered heart says exactly what it needs to. Quietly confident. Permanently summer.

Properly made

180 g/m² cotton. Organic corduroy. Brushed cotton caps. We obsess over weight, feel, and finish because you shouldn't have to replace something that was made right the first time.

Eco-conscious always

Small runs. Organic materials. Removable labels for reuse. We care about the planet we're named after — the one that gives us our summers — and we make choices that reflect that.
